More About Medical Services

Medical Services

Zimbabweans in the diaspora bear a lot of responsibility for the wellbeing of our relatives back home. The majority of us have to ensure the material and health wellbeing of at least several people back home. While it is easy to send money for groceries and bills it becomes a bit harder when health is involved not least because of the varied and interdependent variables about concern about the quality and timeliness of care and well as the uncertainty of what exactly is happening. All too often the cost can be inflated either intentionally or unintentionally. At MUTI UK Ltd we have had our EUREKA moment, we have found a way of streamlining this tumultuous process into a few simple but smart , safe and dependable steps. Our medical personnel trained in Zimbabwe but currently work full time in the UK, because of their Zimbabwe experience they maintain professional and friendly relations with a lot of professional back home. We have found a way of using this network to guarantee good medical care for our relatives back home. The service is not only translational but imparts on the quality and standards of management of the care that our relatives receive thereby bringing world-class medical input literally to the doorstep. The service is accessed in by these easy steps 1) If you have a relative back home requiring medical input simply contact us via our website/Facebook page/text/call with your query and we will send you a request for information. 2) Once we have received further information it will be passed on to our doctor based here in the UK who will then advise on the next step. If it is something that can be sorted within the professional network of MUTI practitioners in Zimbabwe we will provide you with an initial quotation for a consultation for your relative. 3) If you chose to proceed we will ask you to make your relative aware that our doctors back home will be getting in touch to arrange an appointment once you have settled the initial consultation fees. 4) You relative will be seen by our doctor back home and asked to sign a disclosure consent that allows us to discuss their care with the said doctor. 5) After the consultation, we will provide you with a summary of the working diagnosis and quotations for any further prescriptions or tests deemed necessary. 6) We aim to keep you posted timeously at every review appointment The service is in evolution and we keep improving with experience, we have already helped a lot of people and are motivated by the good coming out of this collaboration


More About Pharmaceutical Services

Pharmaceutical Services

At MUTI UK Ltd we have a wide network of pharmacies in Zimbabwe. We have a close professional and cordial relationship with the pharmacists running these enterprises back home. Because of this network we can literally source and supply virtually any prescription in Zimbabwe and for those in remote areas we can even courier the medications to any destination in the country.


There are 2 ways in which this service can be accessed:


1) If your relative already has a prescription whether it is for acute medications or one for their repeat medications we can help source the medications as long as we are satisfied with the validity and medical soundness of said script.

2) For those patients who use our medical services, we are committed to supplying all the medications and treatment our Zimbabwe based medical personnel will prescribe.

​

As a professional service, we adhere to world standards and only supply medication on valid prescriptions which need to have been recently signed by a registered doctor or prescriber. We are happy to have sight of these prescriptions using different platforms (email, text, whatsApp etc) and always crosscheck the validity with our pharmacists on the ground before supplying.

We will always give you a quotation in advance and if needs be we can shop for a better deal for you from our vast network.

In special circumstances, we do have the facility to export medications from the UK if they are not available back home.

Our professional and friendly services are always ready to answer any medication queries and we do signpost you to other services if we cannot meet your requirements.

Our motivation is to translate high-level medical care into the Zimbabwean context to afford our relatives the best care possible
